CAREERS

From finding a job to applying to (hopefully) success, the University careers team are a great resource. They can help with CVs and Cover Letters, have plenty of tips on hand to help with interview nerves, and know about the best local opportunities for part-time work and gradate positions. Saving this, below is some useful websites to make a start on helping you get that dream job!

Finding a Job

https://www.prospects.ac.uk/graduate-jobs https://www.indeed.co.uk/ https://www.gradcracker.com/

Work experience can be a handy way to gain skills that employers look for. https://www.allaboutcareers.com/careers-advice/work-experience/how-to-get-work-experience https://www.prospects.ac.uk/jobs-and-work-experience/work-experience-and-internships

VCs
